How I Build Data Systems

Patterns I've applied across enterprise analytics platforms, ERP migrations, and cloud-native data systems.

Architecture with Intent

I design data systems to scale over time—not just to meet immediate reporting needs. My work focuses on building clear architectural layers, reusable models, and governed analytics foundations that teams can confidently build on.

Translating Complexity

Much of my work involves untangling legacy systems and fragmented business logic. I translate complex, high-risk environments into modern, maintainable data and reporting solutions without disrupting day-to-day operations.

Built for Real Use

The systems I build are meant to be used, not just delivered. I prioritize self-service access, clear metric definitions, and intuitive data models that enable hundreds of users to explore and trust the data independently.

Measured Impact

I focus on outcomes, not just implementations—reducing processing time, improving reliability, lowering infrastructure costs, and increasing adoption across teams and departments.
